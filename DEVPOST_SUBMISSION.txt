PROJECT DESCRIPTION
─────────────────────────────────────────

## The Problem

Financial crime and cybercrime no longer operate in silos. A single fraudulent transaction today may involve a politically exposed person (AML risk), routing through a crypto wallet via a North Korean IP address (cyber risk), destined for a sanctioned country (screening risk) — yet most compliance platforms treat these as separate alerts in separate systems.

The result: compliance teams are overwhelmed with false positives, genuine threats slip through the cracks, and money mule networks operate undetected across hundreds of accounts.

There is no affordable, unified platform that:
- Converges AML and cyber signals into a single coherent risk score
- Detects coordinated mule rings from real transaction graph data
- Produces board-ready intelligence — not just raw alerts

## Our Solution: CyberAML Shield

CyberAML Shield is a full-stack financial intelligence platform that scores transactions across **24 risk factors in 8 signal domains**, detects money mule networks using **Neo4j graph analytics**, and delivers executive-grade intelligence via **Google Gemini AI**.

### How It Works

**1. CAML Risk Score Engine (24 factors · 8 domains)**
Every transaction is scored 0–200 across:
- CYBER: IP/billing mismatch, login attempts, new device fingerprinting
- AML: Source of funds, customer type, beneficial ownership layers
- GEO: Destination country, counterparty country risk
- BEHAV: Velocity, structuring patterns, behavioral change detection
- SCRN: Sanctions screening, adverse media, PEP status, law enforcement match

When AML and Cyber signals co-occur, a **convergence multiplier (up to ×1.50)** elevates the score — reflecting the real-world compounding of risk.

**2. Bulk CSV Scanner (Enterprise Batch Processing)**
Upload any bank export or core banking dump — no fixed template required. Our intelligent column auto-mapper recognises 100+ field name variations. Scan thousands of transactions with a live progress bar, then get a Gemini AI executive summary written at Chief AML Officer level. Export flagged results for investigation.

**3. Mule Radar — Neo4j Live Graph Detection**
Scored transactions are pushed into a Neo4j AuraDB property graph as Account nodes and SENT_TO relationships. Three Cypher queries detect suspicious patterns in real-time:
- **Sanctioned Hub:** Multiple accounts transacting to the same sanctioned country
- **IP Geo Cluster:** Accounts sharing a high-risk IP origin country
- **PEP High-Value:** Politically exposed persons sending large cross-border transactions

Detected rings are rendered as interactive D3.js force-directed network graphs with Gemini AI narrative analysis.

**4. Gemini AI Integration**
- Per-transaction: plain-English explanation of why a score is elevated
- Ring analysis: investigative narrative on detected mule networks
- Bulk summary: board-level executive brief citing actual statistics and top-5 risks

KEY FEATURES & INNOVATION
─────────────────────────────────────────

**What makes CyberAML Shield genuinely novel:**

1. **AML + Cyber Convergence Scoring** — No existing open platform combines cybersecurity signals (device fingerprinting, IP geolocation, login anomalies) with AML signals (PEP, sanctions, source of funds) in a single score. The convergence multiplier is the key innovation.

2. **Schema-free Bulk Processing** — Most AML tools require a rigid input template. Our 100+ alias auto-mapper works with any bank export format out of the box — practical for real compliance teams.

3. **Real Graph-based Ring Detection** — Unlike mock visualisations, our Mule Radar runs live Cypher queries against an actual Neo4j graph built from your own transaction data. The rings shown are real patterns, not generated demos.

4. **Gemini-powered Intelligence Layer** — AI doesn't just flag — it explains. Compliance officers get plain English rationale and board-ready summaries, drastically reducing time-to-decision.

5. **End-to-End in One Platform** — From raw CSV upload → risk scoring → graph ingestion → ring detection → AI brief → export, the entire compliance workflow lives in one interface.
